Dottify.me Privacy Policy

Revised January 3, 2014

Dottify.me is a public project for the Transgender/Transsexual and Gender Variant communities. As members of this community, we understand the substantial concerns of members of our community about privacy, security and confidentiality. In our design, implementation and management of the application and the content it collects, we will always do our utmost to ensure that the trust placed in us is well founded.

Dottify.me is a tool to facilitate research for the Trans* and Gender Non-conforming communities. It is an open-source and open knowledge based project and will likely allow validated and verified members to collect data for beneficial research purposes. However the principles of user control over matters of privacy and disclosure will be enforced as a baseline to ensure that the fundamental contract between users and researchers is always respected. Dottify.me may publish results collected from the site, in various digital and/or non-digital formats,. In line with the policies below, any such publication will only be statistical in nature and any individually identifying data or data combinations will not be publicly published.

If you become aware any security breaches or vulnerabilities in the application or site or have suggestions for enhanced security measures or policies, please contact admin@dottify.org.

Specific Privacy Policies

  • We will never sell, trade or give the information on the site to any person or organization without carefully vetting the reliability of that person or organization.
  • We will never provide detail information (not statistical results) to any commercial organization.
  • We will take care to ensure that any query, filtering or report capabilities of the application will not allow the identity of an individual user to be determined.
  • We will not sell advertising or use the information for commercial targeted marketing. We may list sponsoring organizations and show their logos but not push products.
  • We will not ask and will not allow any member survey to ask questions which will, in our opinion, breach the anonymity of users directly or using common statistical techniques.
  • All user passwords and similar security information will be maintained in strongly encrypted formats
  • Great care will be used in securing the data back end from intrusion, hacking, injection and other common techniques for stealing data
  • If we believe the site or its data security has been or may be compromised, we will immediately secure the data and disable the site until we are confident of site integrity
Note: Dottify.me does not current support 3rd party (membership) researchers to collect data through the site. Before any such features are activated and before Dottify.me releases any raw (non cleansed/deidentified data) to 3rd parties, we will publish our policies and procedures for authenticating and validating 3rd parties and their requests and monitoring use of data from Dottify.me

Suggestions and comments rearding our policy are welcomed. Please direct correspondence to admin@dottify.org.
